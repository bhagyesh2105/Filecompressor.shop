Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Data Collection

We respect your privacy. NanoCompress does not upload your files to any server. All file compression happens locally within your web browser. Your images and PDFs never leave your device.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track basic usage (e.g., how many people visit). This data is anonymous.

3. Contact

If you have questions, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
